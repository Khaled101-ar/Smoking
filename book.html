book1:The easy way to stop smoking

"The Easy Way to Stop Smoking" by Allen Carr is a guide designed to help smokers quit in an easy, straightforward way. Unlike traditional methods that rely on willpower or nicotine replacement therapies (such as patches or gum), Carr's approach focuses on changing the way smokers think about smoking, addressing the psychological addiction rather than just the physical dependency on nicotine.
